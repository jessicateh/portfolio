Guess and Win
With Shopee

Role Interface Design
Client Shopee

Shopee is mobile-centric marketplace where users can browse, shop and sell on the go. To differentiate itself from competitors, Shopee creates weekly games for users to collect points which can be redeemed while shopping. My task is to design the components of the interface in each stage, while evaluating the scenarios of the game.

Assuming rewards and price are the key elements in Guess and Win, I choose the visual of a price tag to feature the product and details. The irregular shape of the button, is to entice users to take the next step and to give a feeling of surprise. “Energy” spending is similar to “amount of lives” in game mechanic. Replicating this mechanism, the bar will turn red if users spend more than 3 energy- it is also an element to alarm users to think before making a decision.

While doing some study case on games, I found out triangles and squares are commonly used to excite users. Such shapes are to call for attention. Colors wise, I want to create an association between Shopee and the game itself. But instead of using Shopee’s primary color, I pick Orange as the dominant color and Purple to compliment it.
